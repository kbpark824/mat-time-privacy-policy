Mat Time Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Mat Time ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Email Address: Used for account creation, login, and communication.
  • User Profile Information: Includes name, belt rank, training preferences, and settings.
  • Training Session Data: Includes session duration, notes, tags, dates, and other details you choose to log.
  • Competition Results (Pro users only): Events, results, opponent names, weight class, etc.
  • Seminar Attendance (Pro users only): Names, dates, and locations of attended seminars.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: IP address, device type, operating system, app version, and time of access (via server logs).
  • Analytics: We may collect aggregated usage metrics to improve app performance and user experience.

1.3 Subscription & Payment Data

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect transaction-related data such as:

  • Subscription tier
  • Purchase date
  • Renewal/cancellation status
  • Platform (iOS/Android)

We do not collect or store any credit card or payment processing information. All payments are handled securely by Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

  • Create and manage your user account
